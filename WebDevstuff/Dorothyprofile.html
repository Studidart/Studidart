Character Profile: Dorothy Gale

Basic Information

Full Name: Dorothy Gale

Age: 11

Home: Kansas, USA

Occupation: Student / Adventurer

Appearance

Hair: Brown, usually worn in pigtails

Eyes: Blue

Clothing: A blue gingham dress with a white blouse, often paired with red slippers

Personality Traits

  • Brave: Faces unknown challenges head-on.
  • Kind-hearted: Shows empathy and compassion.
  • Determined: Resolute in her quest to return home.
  • Optimistic: Maintains a hopeful outlook.

Background

Dorothy lives on a farm in Kansas with her Aunt Em and Uncle Henry. After a tornado transports her to the magical land of Oz, she embarks on a quest to return home, guided by the belief that “there’s no place like home.”

Key Relationships

  • Toto: Dorothy’s loyal dog.
  • Scarecrow: Seeks a brain; values intelligence.
  • Tin Man: Desires a heart; symbolizes compassion.
  • Cowardly Lion: Seeks courage; represents inner strength.
  • Glinda: The Good Witch who helps Dorothy return home.

Notable Quotes

“There's no place like home.”

“Toto, I have a feeling we’re not in Kansas anymore.”

Journey

Dorothy's adventure begins when she is swept away by a tornado to the Land of Oz. Along the way, she meets friends who join her on a quest to see the Wizard, who she believes can help her return to Kansas. They face various challenges and learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of home.

Symbolism

Dorothy's journey represents the quest for self-discovery and the idea that the answers to our desires often lie within ourselves. Her red slippers symbolize the power of choice and the journey back to one’s roots.
